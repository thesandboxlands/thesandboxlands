Are there limits?

Total allocated lands on the Polygon network is limited to 500. Each wallet has a mint limit of 5 lands during this expansion. If you have existing land(s) on the Ethereum network, it will not be counted towards your limit.

Can I select the location of my land?

Minting will not give you an option to choose where your land will be. If you want to buy a specific land, you can do so by buying from other players on Opensea market or www.sandbox.game website.

I am late, when will be there another sale?

This sale was part of our migration to the Polygon Network. We don't have plans to expand The Sandbox area at the moment. You can follow @TheSandboxGame on Twitter and join our Discord channel to be informed about any future plans.

Does land ownership on Polygon contribute towards The Sandbox Season 2 Raffle?

Yes! Regardless of which network your land is in, you will earn +40 raffle tickets for Season 2 Alpha Pass Raffle per land (capped at 5 lands per wallet) and this will you guarantee rewards to up to 1,000 mSAND

Which cryptocurrencies I can use to buy and sell?

You can buy lands only using Ethereum and Matic from this sale. Afterwards, users can sell their lands in any currency they want.
